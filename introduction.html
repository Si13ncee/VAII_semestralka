Introduction

Cooldowns

The complexity of blood death knights comes to properly managing and planning defensive cooldowns, which they have a lot of, compared to other tanks in the game.

Name Description
Death Strike Focuses dark power into a strike that deals (46.4256% of Attack power) Physical damage and heals you for 25.00% of all damage taken in the last 5 sec, minimum 7.0% of maximum health.
Vampiric Blood Embrace your undeath, increasing your maximum health by 30% and increasing all healing and absorbs received by 30% for 10 sec.
Icebound Fortitude Your blood freezes, granting immunity to Stun effects and reducing all damage you take by 30% for 8 sec.
Rune Tap Reduces all damage taken by 20% for 4 sec.
Anti-Magic Shell Surrounds you in an Anti-Magic Shell for 5 sec, absorbing up to [Total health * 30 / 100 * (1 + Versatility) * 1.4 * 1.15] magic damage and preventing application of harmful magical effects. Damage absorbed generates Runic Power.
Lichborne Draw upon unholy energy to become Undead for 10 sec, increasing Leech by 10%, reducing damage taken by 15%, and making you immune to Charm, Fear, and Sleep.
Dancing Rune Weapon Summons a rune weapon for 8 sec that mirrors your melee attacks and bolsters your defenses. While active, you gain 40% parry chance.

Utility

We can divide our utility into two parts. One being a general utility, e.g. crowd control, and the other into party utility, which helps our team to survive or deal more damage. Here is the list of our utility skills.
Name Description
Death Grip Harnesses the energy that surrounds and binds all matter, drawing the target toward you.
Blinding Sleet Targets in a cone in front of you are blinded, causing them to wander disoriented for 5 sec. Damage may cancel the effect. When Blinding Sleet ends, enemies are slowed by 50% for 6 sec.
Asphyxiate Lifts the enemy target off the ground, crushing their throat with dark energy and stunning them for 5 sec.
Anti-magic Zone Places an Anti-Magic Zone that reduces spell damage taken by party or raid members by 20%. The Anti-Magic Zone lasts for 8 sec or until it absorbs (Total health * 1.5 * (1 + Versatility)) damage.
Chains of Ice Shackles the target with frozen chains, reducing movement speed by 70% for 8 sec.
